# Churros — Product Vision

> **Tagline:**  
> **Like chores, but sweeter.**

---

# Overview

**Churros** is a shared household management application designed for families, couples, and roommates.

Its purpose is to simplify everyday household coordination by providing a single shared space for chores, shopping lists, notes, reminders, and events.

Rather than feeling like a productivity tool, Churros is designed to feel like a natural extension of home—a digital fridge door where everyone contributes to keeping the household running smoothly.

---

# The Problem

Managing a household often happens across multiple disconnected channels:

- "Can you buy milk?"
- Sticky notes on the fridge
- Messages in WhatsApp
- Calendar reminders
- Separate shopping apps
- To-do list applications

Information becomes scattered, tasks are forgotten, and responsibility becomes unclear.

Traditional task management applications solve this problem poorly because they are designed for work, not home.

Household management should feel collaborative—not corporate.

---

# The Solution

Churros creates a shared digital home where every household member can:

- Assign chores
- Complete recurring tasks
- Share shopping lists
- Leave notes
- Plan household events
- Receive reminders
- Stay synchronized in real time

Everything belongs to the household instead of an individual.

The family becomes the central entity of the application.

---

# The Philosophy

The name **Churros** comes from a playful transformation:

```
Chores
    ↓
Churros
```

The message behind the brand is simple:

> Doing small household chores together creates more time for sweet moments together.

Instead of focusing on work, the application focuses on the reward:

- less stress
- fewer misunderstandings
- better communication
- more quality time

---

# The Digital Fridge

The central metaphor behind Churros is **the family fridge**.

For decades, refrigerators have naturally become the communication hub of a home.

People leave:

- sticky notes
- shopping lists
- reminders
- invitations
- children's drawings
- calendars

Churros transforms this familiar experience into a modern digital product.

The application should evoke the feeling of looking at an organized fridge covered with colorful notes—without literally resembling one.
